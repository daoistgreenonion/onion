The forest path was rather well-lit and peaceful, it looked decepively safe with warm sunlight coming through in generous amounts as if to signal the absence of danger.

![full A panoramic view of the ancient woods <https://unsplash.com/...>](/illustrations/forest.jpg)

After walking for hours, just as he felt like he was running out of daylight time soon, but before the sky showed any actual signs of twilight, the wanderer who periodically climbed nearby trees to observe his surroundings spotted what looked like a top of a tower slightly to the side, near the mountainside. 

This was goood luck. He stared making markings to lead him back to the forest path, and went off the path toward the structure. Soon, he was delighted to see Warding Stones.

![left A ruin amid the dark forest](/illustrations/stone.jpg)

They were overgrown with greenery and the light of runes carved into them was dim, but it was far better than nothing, and this level of protection would at least ward off any nuisances.

He even found a trace of paved way, and trailing it, found an old delapidated ruin ahead.

It was overgrown with greenery and seemingly long-abandoned, with no traces of recent activity on or around it.

![right](/illustrations/ruin.jpg)

After cautiously observing his surroundings, he decided to carefully press on deeper into the interior of the ruin. Until he found an entrance into a more intact building.
