---
title: "Classification System"
---

Demonic Beasts, Spiritual Plants, Pills, Forging Materials, Spiritual Treasures, etc are ranked by Grade, Tier and Level

Everything is first ranked by grade, with each each grade corresponding to a Great Realm of cultivation.

---

Each Grade is divided into the number of tiers there is of Major Realms in the corresponding Great Realm of cultivation. 

So, in the 1st Great Realm "Nine Mortal Realms" there are 9 tiers  from 1st to 9th.

---

Each Tier is divided into 3 Levels: low, mid and high. 
Low would roughly correspond to the first 3 minor realms in the given major realm. 
Mid would roughly correspond to the 4,5,6 minor realms in the given major realm. 
High would roughly correspond to 7,8,9 minor realms in the given major realm.
