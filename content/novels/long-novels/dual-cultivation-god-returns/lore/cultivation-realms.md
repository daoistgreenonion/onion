title: "Cultivation Realms"
---

Cultivation Realms are divided into:

-Great Realms

-Major Realms (the number is reflected in the Great Realm they belong to)

-Minor Realms (there are always 9 of them in each Major Realm)
1-9 level

***

Minor Realms
Advancements in Minor Realms are simply the various stages of the same transformation. It indicates the completeness of transformation progress.
They are numbered from 1 to 9 because there is a measurable difference, but also because 9 is a symbolic number that signifies the end of a full cycle - going up from 9 would inevitably bring one to 0 from where one has to climb up to 9 for another cycle again.
This also is tied very strongly to the number of Major Realms in the first Great Realm, the Nine Mortal Realms - as they are often called Nine Mortal Transformations and a cycle of a Mortal transcending their being. 
Major Realms
Advancements in Major Realms are a transition between different transformations themselves. They bring qualitative and quantitative changes to the body, mind, and soul of the cultivator, as well as expand their abilities beyond that of a normal human. 
Great Realms
The advancement into a Great Realm is what is called an Ascension, a fundamental elevation of a cultivator as a being from one to another, allowing a new set of transformations to be possible within them. Each ascension requires a cultivator to remove one of the so-called 'shackles' of the Heavenly Daos

[[collapsible: Nine Mortal Realms]]

[[collapsible: Body Transformation Realm]]

The initial step in which the practicioner absorbs Spiritual Qi into their body, tansforming it with the Spiritual Qi, and greatly enhance their physical prowess.

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Qi Gathering Realm]]

The cultivator can gather Spiritual Qi and store it into their Dantian, they can then use this energy to power and enhance the movements of their body.

[[/collapsible]]


[[collapsible: Qi Condensation Realm]]

The usage of Spiritual Qi is the same as in the Qi Gathering Realm, but it is condensed, allowing for much more power to be exerted using it, and the amount of Spiritual Qi that could be stored increases. Throughout the realm the Spiritual Qi is going through a process of change that allows it not to dissipate in the outside world.


[[/collapsible]]


[[collapsible: Qi Manifestation Realm]]

The practitioner's cultivation affects their Root of Longevity, the first realm to increase a cultivator's lifespan (~500 yrs) and the first to partially alleviate mortal needs of hunger, thirst and sleep. The practitioner also gains access to their Soul Sea inside their Niwan Palace. Able to release and manipulate Spiritual Qi outside their body.

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Revolving Qi Realm]]

The practitioner forms a vortex of Spiritual Qi in their Dantian that naturally circulates Spiritual Qi inside their body without conscious control. Improved speed or reaction and acceleration of Spiritual Qi. Further alleviation of mortal needs and longer lifespan. Each level exponentially increases the speed and intensity of the vortex, as well as the density of the Spiritual Qi in it.

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Foundation Building Realm]]

At the gate of the Foundation Building Realm the vortex of Spiritual Qi reaches high speeds and density that begin to form crystallizations, like sand. Throughout the Foundation Building Realm the practitioner then condenses crystals. In each level one crystal is formed. These crystals are not strictly speaking Spiritual Qi in its pure form, as they are not spent when the cultivator empties their reserves of Spiritual Qi, but a condensation of the practitioner's cultivation base. By the 9th level of Foundation Building Realm, a total of nine crystals will be formed. This stage is one of the most resource-heavy of the Nine Mortal Realms, as there is no confirmed upper limit on how much resources one can pour into creating the Foundation Crystals. 

The process of forming the crystals is also complicated, and requires much thought and effort put into it, improperly formed crystals can collapse and cause loss of a Minor Realm, in severe cases internal injuries and even up to lethal results. But perhaps more importantly, the quality and composition of Foundation  Crystals greatly affected future cultivation.

The ability cultivator gained in this realm was a massive boost to the reserves of Spiritual Qi, and perhaps more importantly the ability to layer and structure Spiritual Qi as it was stored, allowing to create pre-formed patterns and structures while the Spiritual Qi was stored

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Core Formation Realm]]

The practitioner combines the crystals formed in the Foundation Building Realm into a spherical structure, and then gradually connect them together building first bridges and then solidifying those bonds until they form a complete core. This becomes a fundamental part of any practitioner's cultivation and has tremendous effects on the future. The quality of the core at this stage is determined not only by the quality of the crystals formed in the Foundation Building Realm, but also by the combination pattern and the connecting. This gives ability to release and control far greater volumes of Spiritual Qi at once, and at the same time gives far greater precision of control, since the cultivation base is concentrated in one core, it allows for much finer and minute manipulation of Spiritual Qi.  The core consolidated and stabilized Spiritual Qi of the practitioner, its flow becoming smooth and even, without fluctuations and flaws inherent in the natural state of Qi. Meaning that any technique used by a Core Formation Realm cultivator would become two to three times stronger than of any cultivator below that realm, even if all other conditions were even, including but not limited to quantity of Spiritual Qi used and its potency, as well as skill of manipulating. This is the minimum required level to start practicing most Supreme Cultivation Methods. 

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Core Reformation Realm]]

Core Reformation Realm requires only a bit more resources than the Core Formation Realm. Despite that it is one of the biggest hurdles on one's way to Mortal Transcendence Realm, because it is full of danger for the cultivator. The practitioner would saturate their Core with energy until its absolute limits, and then it would start to form cracks under pressure. The cracks would reveal inherent flaws in the core, that formed mostly not because of the bad structure or flaws in methods of its creation, although in some cases it was that, but because of the nature of Spiritual Qi that had inconsistencies and imperfections within its tiniest particles. Then one would completely crush their core along the cracks, and reform it from the shards into a new, more perfected version. However, the practitioner had to be very careful with the process as crushing the core was normally akin to suicide. It was only possible due to the mysterious state one entered when doing so in the Core Reformation Realm. And even despite that, many lives were lost in this Realm. This was where the disparity of legacy was in vivid display between those with rich heritage and resources and those rising from the bottom in hopes of attaining everything. Since there were various techniques and methods of Core Reformation, that allowed a near perfect survival rate, as well as alchemy pills that pushed that 'near perfect' to true perfect.

This was also a peculiar Realm where practitioners did not have to go through all nine Minor Realms in order to advance to the Mortal Transcendence Realm. As soon as the core could withstand enough pressure to advance, be it only after only two reformations, or five, which varied randomly for every individual, it was possible to skip to the next Major Realm. But it was a general consensus among those of influence and power, to not consider those who did not go through all nine reformations in the Core Reformation Realm an apt force beyond Mortal Realms. There was also no immediate giant leap in strength between a Core Formation and Core Reformation Realm expert, and almost no difference between those who reformed their core more times, although there were differences. Thus many less fortunate people of limited means often only risked to attempt the exact number of reformations required for them to attempt a breakthrough to the Mortal Transcendence Realm. The more powerful and impressive the core is, the more complex and dangerous the procedure of Core Reformation. There was another aspect of the process that was crucial, which was condensation and compression of all residual parts of cultivation within Dantian into the core. Even as one completed their core when they reached the gate of Core Reformation Realm, there was still a vortex of Spiritual Qi rotating around it in their Dantian which was first formed in Revolving Qi Realm, with some small crystallizations within the rotating mist of Spiritual Qi left from the Foundation Building Realm. With each initial breaking of the core in the first three Minor Realms, it is this vortex and small crystal particles that would be filling the gaps. The core itself would first rotate at a slower speed than the vortex, but with each cycle of reformation in the middle three Minor Realms its speed would rise to eventually reach and then surpass the speed of rotation of the vortex of Spiritual Qi. That speed would then cause a condensation and compression of the core itself in the latter three Minor Realms, as the core would become two times smaller in size by the end. By the peak of Core Reformation Realm, that vortex of Spiritual Qi would be completely gone, and only the rapidly spinning core would remain, with concentrated streams of Spiritual Qi spreading from it toward the meridians.

[[/collapsible]]
