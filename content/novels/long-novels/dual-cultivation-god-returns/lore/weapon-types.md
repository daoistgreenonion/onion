[[collapsible: Sword]]

Alternative names: Jian

A straight two-bladed sword. Can vary in length, and aesthetics. 
When a sword is mentioned in the novel without any other discriptors, it is generally this type. 

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Saber ]]

Alternative names: DaDao

A one-bladed weapon generally considered heavier than a sword

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Spear]]

Alternative names: Mao Spear

A spear with the spear blade in a shape of a small sword. When a spear is mentioned in the novel in the hands of the cultivator it is generally this one, unless specified otherwise.

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Army Spear ]]

Alternative names: Qiang Spear

A spear commonly used by foot soldiers.

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Guandao Spear]]

Alternative names: Reclining Moon Spear

A polearm with a broad curved blade (single edge) used mostly for cleaving and not piercing.

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Halberd]]

The chinese version of heavy pole weapon, with crescent blades on the sides, there are variations where the blades are attached with more space in between with two separate uniting parts instead of one in the middle.

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Ruler ]]

Alternative names: TieChi (Iron Ruler)

Style maces, with generally blunt edges, considerable size and weight.

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Ox-tailed sword ]]

Alternative names: NiuWeiDao

A broadsword with a heavier and broader tip commonly issued to the soldiers

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Chakram  ]]

A circular disc, with the outer part being a blade, there are those that have connecting parts within the circle and simply circular ones. It is usually held by the handle which can be on the side or on the connecting part within a circle 
Considered to be part-throwing part-close combat weapon. 

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Iron Fan  ]]

An unconventional and discreet close combat weapon, the design can conceal one as a normal fan in some cases. There can also be a sharp version. ⚠️  V.4  Sui Luxiao specifically uses the blunt one

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Nine Ring Broadsword  ]]

Alternative names: Nine Ring Dao

A variation on the Saber (DaDao) which is typically a bit smaller in size, and has nine rings on the back side.

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Monk's Spade  ]]

A traditional Chinese polearm consisting of a long shaft with a flat spade-like blade on one end and a crescent-shaped blade on the other. Originally carried by Buddhist monks for burial rites and self-defense, it evolved into a martial weapon used in Shaolin Kung Fu. The crescent blade was designed to fend off animals, while the spade could be used for both combat and ceremonial purposes. It was also said that the spade could be used like a shovel in order to allow monks traveling through the countryside to perform good deeds like repairing roads and shovel dirt.

[[/collapsible]]
