### Main Techniques

[[collapsible: Dual Extremes Infinite Union Art]]
Wu Long's main Dual Cultivation technique.


- 1st layer, the base layer of the technique outlining its principles

[[lock 63]]
- 2nd layer, Union of Harmonic Qi, a permanent link of connection between the Yang Qi of Wu Long and the Yin Qi of his ladies 
[[/lock]]

[[lock 259]]
- 3rd layer, the Union of Harmonic Roots, a permanent link between the Yin and Yang Roots, that allowed for incremental mutual cultivation at all times

[[collapsible: Chaos Refining Body Tempering Art]]
A very powerful technique used by an old powerful clan, that allows to refine any energy and convert it into one useable by the technique practitioner. Even the energy used to hurt the user can be eventually converted into one that strengthens them. Can also be used to temper the body. 
[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Chaos Origin Eyes]]
A powerful Eye technique, not much is currently disclosed in the novel of its capabilities, but its use goes beyond giving insight and greater visual perception. For now, it grants Wu Long unheard level of visul perception far exceeding his cultivation level.

[[collapsible: Spiritual Qi War Art]]
A technique created by Battle God, that allows the user to increase reserves and imrpove quality of their Spiritual Qi, one of the most widely known and used techniques in the Seven Boundless Worlds, one of the reasons cultivators outside of the Three Continents, even in Nine Mortal Realms, are far more formidable foes
[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Dantian ]]
Wu Long's Dantian is one of a kind after the Grandmist.
[[collapsible: Grandmist Core ]]
A core at the center of Wu Long's Dantian, the original core he created to cultivate, prior to the Grandmist Keepsake changing his cultivation path. For now it is a black box mystery
[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Ring of Spiritual Qi ]]
A ring of Spiritual Qi that flows around the Grandmist Core, upon saturation it formed multiple vortexes, similar to the ones formed by cultivators in Revolving Qi Realm. He later gained ability to cultivate the vortexes, as well as to increase their numbers
[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Crystal Core ]]
Technically Wu Long's second core, but for the purposes of pure cultivation, it is the only one, since the first one stopped functioning as a Crystal Core when it turned into a Grandmist Core. Apart from the fact it is an auxiliary core that orbits the Grandmist Core within the Ring of Spiritual Qi, this core is for all intends and purposes the normal core a cultivator would have, and functions absolutely as a normal core should. That said, even more resources were spent on its construction, so it is even more powerful than the first core would have been had it remained a normal core.
[[/collapsible]]
