[[collapsible: Special Physique]]

A special physiology that is mostly naturally occuring and for the most part rare.
There are different types of them, the ones that are revealed in the novel are:
- Variant Physique
- Deviation Physique
- Extreme (Yin/Yang) Physique

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Spiritual Qi]]

The basics of the basics. The basic energy that permeates all creation. It is also a collective name for many energies together. 

[[/collapsible]]

[[collapsible: Reinforced Qi / Profound Qi]]

A special type of Qi that one obtains after advancing into Seven Profound Realms. 
It is Spiritual Qi, but reinforced by the Qi from other Seven Roots , Elemental Qi if one has any, and any special Qi that a cultivator might awaken or obtain - hence the name Reinforced Qi, and called interchangeably Profound Qi since it is wielded by those in Seven Profound Realms. 
 

[[/collapsible]]
