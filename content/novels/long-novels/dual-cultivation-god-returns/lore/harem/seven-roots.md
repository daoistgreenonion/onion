---
title: "Seven Roots"
---

The Root of Power, located in the spine, which governs the strength of physical body.

The Root of Vitality, located in human heart that governs the cultivator's blood flow and body functions.

The Root of Harmony, otherwise known as Yin or Yang Root, located in the lower abdomen below the Dantian.

The Root of Spirituality, otherwise known as Spiritual Qi Root, from which the Dantian grows, producing Spiritual Qi and serving as the core of cultivation.

The Root of Longevity, located in the abdomen above Dantian, governing the lifespan, and in case of cultivators nourished by the Root of Spirituality.

The Root of Awareness, located in the center part of the head behind the Niwan Palace, and serving as the crux of the human senses such as vision, hearing, taste and smell but not limited to that.

The Root of Wisdom, otherwise called the Soul Root, located in the Niwan Palace, from which springs the Soul Sea.

***

All seven roots are connected and are essential for the life and cultivation of a practitioner.
The Seven Roots are considered general knowledge for all cultivators.
They are always listed in the particular order as the two closest Roots in the list have the strongest connection.
Each of the Seven Roots has Essence stored inside, as well as Innate or Pure Qi.

Root of Power has Marrow Essence and Innate/Pure Marrow Qi

Root of Vitality has Blood Essence and Innate/Pure Blood Qi

Root of Harmony has Harmonic (Yin/Yang) Essence and Innate/Pure Harmonic (Yin/Yang) Qi

Root of Spirituality has Spiritual Essence and Innate/Pure Spiritual Qi

Root of Longevity has Life Essence and Innate/Pure Life Qi

Root of Awareness has Mind Essence and Innate/Pure Mind Qi

Root of Wisdom has Soul Essence and Innate/Pure Soul Qi
